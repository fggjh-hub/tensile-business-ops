# Pre-Incorporation: Vet Compliance Partners

## The Objective
You want to hit "Go" on incorporation and have all applications (bank, virtual office, insurance) submitted matching the exact correct details within 60 minutes. You can vet and pick these partners now.

## Step 1: Virtual Office Selection
To keep your home address off the public Companies House register, you must use a Virtual Office from the exact moment you incorporate.
*   **Action:** Go to the website of your chosen provider (e.g., **The Hoxton Mix** or **UK Postbox**). 
*   **Action:** Confirm exactly which package includes *both* "Company Registered Office" and "Director’s Service Address". (Often called the "Privacy Package").
*   **Action:** Bookmark the checkout page. 

## Step 2: Banking Choice
*   **Action:** Decide between **Tide** and **Monzo Business**. 
*   **Action:** Download the chosen app to your phone now. 
*   **Action:** Check their current requirements. Both will ask for your Certificate of Incorporation (which you don't have yet), but you can verify their pricing tiers and integration with accounting software. Note that Tide often requires a selfie video for ID verification.

## Step 3: Contract Preparation
*   **Action:** Go to **Qdos Contractor** (or similar IR35 specialists).
*   **Action:** Navigate to their contract templates section. Identify the "Outside IR35 Consultancy Agreement" template.
*   **Action:** Read the sample clauses if available. Understand how you will need to adjust your scope of work to ensure Mutuality of Obligation (MOO) is firmly rejected.

When you are ready to launch, you will execute Step 1 (Buy Virtual Office), then use that address to incorporate on Gov.uk, and immediately use the resulting incorporation number to execute Step 2 (Open Bank Account).
