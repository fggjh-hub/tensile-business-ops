# Pre-Incorporation: Build Landing Page

## The Objective
You do not need a registered company to build and host your website. Having this live on Day 1 gives your cold outreach immediate credibility. As a developer, do not spend 4 weeks engineering this from scratch. Keep it ruthlessly simple and conversion-focused.

## Step 1: The One-Page Architecture
Your website only needs a single page. 
Use **Next.js** or a static site generator, and host it for free on **Vercel** or **Netlify**.

The page must contain exactly 4 sections:
1.  **The Hero:** "We rescue legacy codebases for UK Digital Agencies. Stop wasting your best developers on maintenance."
2.  **The Problem:** Detail the pain point—launched sites degrading over time, dependency alerts piling up, developers getting frustrated fixing old bugs instead of building new features.
3.  **The Solution (The Two-Product Menu):**
    *   System Health Audit (£500 fixed fee, 4-page written report)
    *   SLA-backed Safety Retainer (£1,500/month per covered core system)
4.  **The CTA:** "Book a 15-minute Discovery Call" linking directly to a **Calendly** or **Cal.com** booking page.

## Step 2: The Positioning Copy
*   **Do not** say "I am a Full Stack Developer."
*   **Do** say "We are a specialised maintenance desk."
The word "We" implies stability and a B2B relationship, which is critical for Outside IR35 compliance.

## Step 3: Launch
1. Push the code to GitHub.
2. Deploy via Vercel/Netlify.
3. Link the custom domain you purchased. 
Nobody visiting the site knows if the Limited Company behind it is legally formed yet or not. By the time you send your first cold email, the site will look like an established, robust business.
