# Pre-Incorporation: Build Target Hit-List

## The Objective
Do not incorporate a business and *then* spend two weeks trying to find people to email. Lead generation is pure administrative hustle. You can build a pristine database of 100 dream clients while waiting to legally launch.

## Step 1: The Spreadsheet
Create a Google Sheet with the following columns:
`Company Name` | `Website` | `Founder/MD Name` | `LinkedIn URL` | `Email Address` | `Personalised Opening Line`

## Step 2: The LinkedIn Search
1. Turn on a free trial of LinkedIn Sales Navigator.
2. Dial in the filters:
   *   **Geography:** United Kingdom
   *   **Headcount:** 11-50 employees (Crucial: smaller means no budget, larger means internal DevOps teams).
   *   **Industry:** Design Services, Digital Marketing, Software Development.
   *   **Keywords:** "Managing Director", "Founder", "Agency".
3. Manually review the results. If a company looks like an agency that builds web products for end-clients, add the Founder to your spreadsheet.

## Step 3: Data Enrichment
You cannot guess email addresses.
1. Sign up for a free tier of **Apollo.io** or **Hunter.io**.
2. Run the founder's name and company domain through the tool to find their verified B2B email address (e.g., `james.smith@designagency.co.uk`).
3. **The Secret Weapon:** Fill out the `Personalised Opening Line` column for every single lead right now. Go to their agency website and look at their recent case studies. Write an opening line like: *"I loved the recent interactive React portal you built for [Client Name]."* 

When you launch, you will upload this sheet into Lemlist or Instantly and have a highly personalised cold outreach campaign ready to fire on Day 1.
