# Pre-Incorporation Master Checklist

Setting up as much as possible before officially incorporating (Phase 1) is a very smart move. The moment you incorporate, the clock starts ticking on Companies House filings and HMRC deadlines. By front-loading the remaining phases, you can hit the ground running on "Day 1" without burning cash on insurance and accounting software prematurely.

Here is exactly what you can do across all phases right now, without a registered company, without insurance, and without business bank accounts.

## 1. Brand, Naming & Landing Page 
*(From Phase 1)*
*   **Verify Availability:** Check your desired business name against the Companies House register and cross-reference it with `.co.uk` domain availability.
*   **Secure the Domain:** Buy the domain now using your personal card (you will expense this back to the business later). 
*   **Build the Website:** Design, code, and deploy a single-page landing page (e.g., using Next.js on Vercel or a simple static site) that strictly pitches the "Legacy Code Rescue" angle. 

## 2. Vet Compliance Partners
*(From Phase 1)*
*   **Choose Virtual Office:** Compare Hoxton Mix, UK Postbox, etc. Decide which package you will buy on Day 1 to protect your home address.
*   **Choose Financials:** Decide between Tide, Monzo, or Starling. 
*   **Review IR35 Contracts:** Look at the exact "Outside IR35" contract templates from Qdos Contractor so you know what you are buying.

## 3. Build the "System Health Audit" Product
*(From Phase 2)*
*   **Create the Template:** Design the 3-to-4 page PDF template you will hand to clients in Canva/Notion.
*   **Refine the Tooling:** Master `npm audit`, the Snyk CLI, and Google Lighthouse.
*   **Time Box It:** Practice generating a complete audit report in under 3 hours.

## 4. Build the "Professional Desk" Infrastructure
*(From Phase 2)*
*   **Set up Tooling:** Create the company Slack workspace, set up Linear for your ticketing, and configure your `@tensileinfrastructure.co.uk` email address via Google Workspace.
*   **Draft Internal Docs:** Write down exactly what your asynchronous SLA means internally (e.g. checking emails between 6 PM - 9 PM exclusively).

## 5. Prepare the YunoJuno Profile Assets
*(From Phase 3)*
*   Write your biography in a text file focusing on the *"Specialist in Legacy Code Rescue"* angle.
*   List out 2-3 "Before and After" mini case studies outlining outcomes.

## 6. Build Your Target Hit-List
*(From Phase 3)*
*   Get a free trial of LinkedIn Sales Navigator and export the names of 10-50 person UK design agencies.
*   Use Apollo/Hunter.io to find their email addresses. 
*   Write a highly personalised opening line for each of the 100 leads.

## 7. Configure Your Automation Mocks
*(From Phase 4)*
*   Create a dummy React/Node repository on GitHub.
*   Install **Renovate** or **Dependabot**.
*   Master the `renovate.json` configuration so it groups minor updates and runs only on specific days.

## What NOT to do before Incorporation:
*   **Do not send the outbound emails yet.** You have no insurance to protect yourself and no business bank account to take money.
*   **Do not sign B2B Contracts.** You want these signed by the Limited Company entity, not you personally.
