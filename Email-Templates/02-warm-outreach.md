## Notes

- Always open with the connection — do not make them work out why you're emailing.
- The referrer's name in the subject line significantly improves open rates.
- If they showed inbound interest (e.g. filled in a form, emailed hello@), respond the same day.
- Mirror their energy — if they were brief, be brief. If they wrote a lot, match it.
- Do not over-explain the services. They can read the website if they want the detail.
