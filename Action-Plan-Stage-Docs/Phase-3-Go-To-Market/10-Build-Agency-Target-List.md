# Phase 3: Build Agency Target List

## Context
Your absolute best clients are UK-based digital, design, and marketing agencies. They excel at building relationships and designing interfaces, but they often lack the highly technical backend engineering required to maintain a complex app 2 years after launch.

## Defining the Target
You are not looking for massive global agencies (they have 24/7 DevOps teams). You are not looking for solo-founders (they have no budget).

*   **Size:** 10 to 50 employees.
*   **Location:** UK-based (for timezone, currency, and cultural alignment).
*   **Type:** Design Agencies, Web Development Agencies, Marketing/Digital Agencies.

## Executing the Search
Use **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** (you can usually get a 30-day free trial).
1.  **Filters:**
    *   Geography: United Kingdom.
    *   Company Headcount: 11-50.
    *   Industry: Marketing Services, Design Services, IT Services and IT Consulting.
    *   Title: "Managing Director", "Founder", "Technical Director", "CTO".
2.  **The List:** Export 100-200 high-quality profiles to a Google Sheet. You don't need thousands; you need a highly targeted list. 
3.  **Finding Emails:** Use tools like **Apollo.io** or **Hunter.io** to cross-reference their LinkedIn profiles and find their direct professional email addresses. Do not use generic `info@agency.co.uk` addresses.
