# Phase 3: Hit Month 3 Targets

## The Milestone
By the end of Month 3, you are aiming to have completed **3 System Health Audits** and converted at least **1 of those into a recurring Safety Retainer**. 

## Tracking Metrics (KPIs)
To ensure you hit this, track the following metrics weekly in a simple spreadsheet:
1.  **Outreach Volume:** How many highly personalised cold emails did you send? (Target: 20-30/week).
2.  **Audit Conversion Rate:** Out of the discovery calls you had, how many bought the £500 audit?
3.  **Retainer Conversion Rate:** Out of the completed audits, how many converted to the monthly retainer?

## Pivot Points (If you are missing targets)
*   *If you aren't getting discovery calls:* Your cold email list is too broad, or your message is too long. Tighten the list to highly specific technologies (e.g., target exclusively "Shopify Plus Partner" agencies and offer Shopify Liquid/React maintenance).
*   *If you are selling audits but no retainers:* Your audit report might be too aggressively critical of their internal team, causing them to become defensive, or your retainer price is misaligned with the perceived value of the highlighted technical debt.

## Financial Consolidation
If you hit the target (1 retainer at £1,500/month + 3 audits at £500 = £3,000 cash collected so far), ensure this money is sitting in your business bank account. Use FreeAgent to reconcile the VAT (if applicable) and Corporation Tax reserves. Do not touch this money personally yet; leave it in the business to fund Phase 4.
